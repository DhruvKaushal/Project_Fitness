WHAT IS BMI?  BMI is used as a screening tool to indicate whether a person is underweight, overweight, obese or a healthy weight for their height. If a person's BMI is out of the healthy BMI range, their health risks may increase significantly. Also, BMI is just a rough indicator of your current body status. It may or may not always be accurate.
